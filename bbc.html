The Boston Beer Company Work

The work on this page is from my co-op working at the Boston Beer Company. All work was made in photoshop using assets from several places and brought together to be edited. Artwork ©The Boston Beer Company. Other works do not necessarily express the views of Boston Beer.

This cooler wrap was made to wrap a wall in a liquor store. I pulled elements from several different pieces of Angry Orchard POS and packaging to create one cohesive wrap. I unfortunately was not able get actual pictures after installation, so I made a mockup of what it would have looked like.

This poster was made to go along with a grocery store coupon. It incorporated both the Samuel Adams and Angry Orchard brands along with a picture of a pork dinner. The plate of pork was pulled from two different images and edited to be one cohesive plate.

This was made to be hung up in a bowling alley to highlight the Samuel Adams Perfect Pint. This image was edited from a vertical picture of a bowling lane that was doubled and merged to look like one long lane. The ball was edited into the picture as well to make everything feel more realistic.

This poster was made to be hung up in an On-Premise account featuring several Samuel Adams pints on draft. I faced the challenge of making the pints all sit on together while still keeping the perspective as realistic as possible. I think this came out clean and simple, and the sales rep loved it as much as I did!
